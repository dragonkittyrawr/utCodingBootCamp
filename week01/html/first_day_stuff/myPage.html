How to Make a Sandwich

  1. Realize you are hungry and the only solution is to eat a turkey sandwich.
  2. Go to your nearest sandwich preparation location.
  3. Verify you have the required sandwich making materials.
    • Plate
    • Knife
    • Loaf of Sliced Bread
    • Sliced Turkey
    • A condiment like Mayonaise, Mustard, or Miracle Whip (optional)
  4. Grab the loaf of bread (gently!)
  5. Select the two best peices of bread and lay them on the plate.
  6. If you are using a condiment, use the knife to spread your selected condiment on the side of bread facing up.
  7. Select the best pieces of turkey and lay them on the bread.
  8. Take each piece of bread in one hand, and close the sandwich by bringing your hands together.
  9. Eat the Sandwich.
